About

The journey of RCB is more than just cricket. It’s about spirit, loyalty, and the dream to never stop believing. This website is dedicated to celebrating the legacy, memories, and future of Royal Challengers Bangalore

Over the years, RCB has been home to some of the biggest cricketing superstars, including Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, Chris Gayle, and Glenn Maxwell. Known for its aggressive batting line-up, passionate fanbase, and thrilling matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, RCB has always played cricket with heart and hunger.

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a professional franchise cricket team that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 and based in Bangalore, Karnataka, RCB has grown into one of the most popular and exciting teams in the league.

Despite facing many ups and downs, the team has never given up. After coming close several times, RCB finally achieved glory by winning their first IPL title in 2025 — a historic moment that brought joy to millions of fans around the world.

Highest run-scorer in IPL history – Over 7,000 runs (most for a single franchise). 973 runs in a single season (2016) – A record that still stands. 5 IPL centuries – All for RCB. Holds the record for most runs in an IPL partnership (229 runs with AB de Villiers) he Captained RCB from 2013 to 2021.

AB de Villiers, also known as Mr. 360 for his ability to hit the ball to all corners of the ground, joined RCB in 2011 and became an instant fan favorite. His bond with Kohli is one of the strongest in IPL history. Key Records & Achievements: Scored over 4,500 runs for RCB with a strike rate above 150. Hit some of the most unbelievable match-winning knocks, including 129* vs Gujarat Lions. Part of two 200+ partnerships with Kohli – a unique record. Known for his impossible chases and fearless style under pressure.

Legacy: ABD’s innovation, calmness, and ability to entertain made him a cult hero. Even after retiring, RCB fans chant “AB, AB!” in the stands.

Chris Gayle redefined T20 cricket with his raw power and fearless hitting. He joined RCB in 2011 and quickly became a nightmare for bowlers. Key Records & Achievements: Scored the highest-ever individual score in IPL – 175* off 66 balls in 2013. Hit 6 centuries for RCB, the most by any RCB player. Holds the record for most sixes in IPL history – over 350 in total. Won the Orange Cap in 2011 and 2012 for scoring the most runs in those seasons. Legacy: Gayle brought charisma, confidence, and dominance. Every time he walked out to bat, fans expected fireworks – and he usually delivered.
